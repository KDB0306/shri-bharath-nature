A Letter from the Heart

My Dearest Chikkiiii

Thanga Kuttyyyy,

I used to think love was something you had to go looking for. Like it was hiding somewhere and you just had to be patient enough to find it. But then you walked into my life and I realised I had been wrong about everything. Love was never hiding. It was just waiting for the right person to make it make sense.

And then I found those NASA images and I honestly could not breathe for a moment. There we are. Written into the earth itself. Our names carved by rivers and valleys and mountains that have been there for millions of years. Like the planet knew about us before we even knew about each other.

You are the river that runs through everything for me. You make even the ordinary days feel like something worth holding onto. The way you laugh, the way you think, the way you look at the world. I notice all of it and I fall a little more every single time.

I love you across every season, through every storm, from the deepest ocean floor to the highest mountain I can imagine. You are my favourite place on this entire earth. And I am so grateful you exist.

Yours always, Chikkuuu ♡
