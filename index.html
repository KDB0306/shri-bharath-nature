A Letter from the Heart

My Dearest Shri

My love,

I always believed love was something you searched for — in crowded places, in quiet moments, in the spaces between words. But then I found you, and I realised love was never hidden. It was already there, carved into the landscape of this earth, waiting for us to look up.

Look at what the rivers drew. Look at what the mountains etched across the desert sand. Before we were born, the planet wrote our names together. That is not coincidence — that is destiny, written in the oldest language there is.

You are the river that bends gently through my life, shaping everything you touch into something more beautiful. You are the green valley after a long drought, the first light catching snow on a mountainside.

I love you — across every continent, through every season, from the deepest ocean floor to the highest peak. Forever is not long enough, but it is where I will start.

— Yours, Always · Bharath ♡
